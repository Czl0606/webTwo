Care

Equipment Feeding Watering Bonding
The very first thing that is necessary is a coop, then you would also need to purchase feeding and watering pots.
The coop must be insulated and well ventilated with enough room for your chicks! 		They should be provided with plenty of food and water!
You can feed them ready-made commercial poultry feeds.
Other good foods/treats are vegetables, corn, and whole wheat. 		It is necessary to provide fresh and clean water on a regular basis! Remember to always cuddle with your chickens to show some love!
Give them little smooches and hugs.
